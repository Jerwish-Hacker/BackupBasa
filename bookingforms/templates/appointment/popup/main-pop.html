COVID-19 Vaccine Update

{{ title|title }} has been notified by the Kern County Public Health Department of a recall of the Moderna COVID-19 Vaccine. The state of California has recalled LOT #041L20A. This recall has currently depleted the current supply of Moderna COVID-19 vaccines left to be administered by Kern County Public Health to {{ title|title }}.

{{ title|title }} wants to assure patients who received a Moderna COVID Vaccine on January 18th and earlier did not receive a recalled vaccine. However, this has created a supply issue.

Effective 1/19/2021, {{ title|title }} is not taking new vaccine appointment requests. CSV staff is closely working with the Public Health Department on vaccine stock supply, and the appointment system will be back online once the supply is available. Please come back later, visit our website for live updates.
